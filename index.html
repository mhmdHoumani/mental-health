Health Is Wealth

What Is Health:

Health is a state of complete emotional,physical and social well-being.

The most important relation is between health and mental health.

What is Mental Health?

Refers to cognitive, behavioral, and emotional well-being. Any flow leads to mental health disorder.

{ Mental Health Disorder }

Causes

  • Obesity & overweight: Like sedentary lifestyle.
  • Childhood abuse: Like rape
  • Long-term stress: like unemployment
  • Bereavement (losing someone close to you)

Effects

  • Health problems : diabetes
  • Relation-ship difficulties: changing in moods
  • Addiction: Drugs
  • Social Isolation
