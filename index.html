Health Is Wealth

What Is Health:

Health is a state of complete emotional,physical and social well-being.

The most important relation is between health and mental health.

What is Mental Health?

Refers to cognitive, behavioral, and emotional well-being. Any flow leads to mental health disorder.

Mental Health Disorder

Causes

  • Obesity & overweight: Like sedentary lifestyle.
  • Childhood abuse: Like rape
  • Long-term stress: like unemployment
  • Bereavement (losing someone close to you)

Effects

  • Health problems : diabetes
  • Relation-ship difficulties: changing in moods
  • Addiction: Drugs
  • Social Isolation

Healthy Food & Lifestyle

A healthy lifestyle is a way of living that lowers the risk of being seriously ill or dying.

A Healthy lifestyle is considered to be about healthy diet and healthy body care. Each kind of health.
helps keep the body healthy and energetic. Consequently, the person will live a life full of relaxation and free of stress.
Healthy diet is the first side of the gold coin of health, it is based on the reported intake of healthy food.
Health body care is the second side of the gold coin of health which talks about practicing healthy physical activities.
